Welcome to the official **Bearby** blog.

Bearby is a non-custodial, open-source, quantum-resistant wallet that puts you in full control of your keys and your assets across multiple blockchains. Available as a browser extension and mobile app, Bearby lets you send and receive tokens, stake, roll-buy and run nodes, interact with smart contracts, and connect to dApps — all secured by post-quantum cryptography, and all without ever handing over your private keys.

Here you'll find:

- **Release notes** — what's new in every version of the wallet.
- **Guides & tutorials** — step-by-step help for staking, rolls, transfers, and dApp connections.
- **Security** — best practices for keeping your seed phrase and funds safe.
- **Ecosystem news** — updates from Bearby and the wider multi-chain community.

New posts land here regularly. Follow along and stay in control of your crypto.
